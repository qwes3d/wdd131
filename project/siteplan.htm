Nnamotion Site Plan

1. Website Title

Nnamotion

2. Purpose

  • Platform showcasing and promoting construction equipment and building materials
  • Provide detailed information and images of products
  • Allow users to request quotes
  • Highlight company strengths in quality, pricing, and delivery
  • Generate leads and build trust with potential customers

3. Target Audience

  • Construction contractors and subcontractors
  • Building materials wholesalers and retailers
  • Real estate developers
  • DIY builders and engineers

4. Main Pages & Description

Page Description
Home Intro, hero banner, company strengths, featured products
Products Categorized listings of construction equipment and building materials
Services Rentals, delivery, installation, and other services
Quote Form for requesting pricing/estimates
About Us Company background, team details, certifications
Contact Contact form, business info, map
Testimonials Customer reviews and feedback

5. Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #f2c300 (Buttons, highlights, icons)
  • Secondary Color: #2e2e2e (Backgrounds, footers)
  • Accent Color: #ffffff (Text on dark backgrounds)
  • Support Color: (CTAs or highlights - optional)

6. Typography

  • Roboto: Headings and body text
  • Montserrat: Navigation and emphasis

Google Fonts link to include on all pages:

<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Roboto:wght@400;700&family=Montserrat:wght@400;700&display=swap" rel="stylesheet">

7. CSS File Structure

  • styles/normalize.css - Reset browser styles
  • styles/base.css - Mobile-first styles (typography, colors, layout)
  • styles/responsive.css - Media queries for tablets (≥600px) and desktops (≥1024px)

8. Responsive Design Strategy

  • Mobile-first approach
  • Use Flexbox and CSS Grid for layouts
  • Breakpoints at 600px (tablet) and 1024px (desktop)
  • Responsive navigation with highlights

9. Page Layout & Features

  • Header with logo and navigation (hamburger menu on mobile)
  • Hero section with large banner/slider
  • Content sections with semantic HTML, Flexbox/Grid
  • Clean, accessible Quote and Contact forms
  • Footer with contact info and social links
  • Optional: Sticky nav, quote popup, live chat, blog
